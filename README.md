# 🚗 Project Free Roam

Welcome to **Project Free Roam** — a community-driven organization dedicated to building open-source mods and resources for **FiveM**, the popular GTA 5 multiplayer modification framework.

---

## 🎮 What We Do

We create, maintain, and share high-quality **FiveM** resources including:

- 🗺️ Custom game modes and scripts
- 🛠️ Utility libraries and frameworks
- 🚙 Vehicle, map, and gameplay enhancements
- 🤝 Open-source tools for the FiveM / GTA 5 modding community

Our goal is to make FiveM development more accessible, fun, and collaborative for everyone — from first-time scripters to experienced developers.

---

## 🌐 Community

- FiveM runs on the **RAGE Multiplayer** engine and lets players connect to custom dedicated servers running modified GTA 5 gameplay.
- Resources are written primarily in **Lua**, **JavaScript**, and **C#**.
